PU CHEMICAL

Polyurethane (Known as PU or PUR) is a synthetic material (Polymer) that is used in a wide range of diverse industries including, among others, automotive, furniture, construction, refrigeration, and footwear industries.

Baalbaki started in the early nineties the first Polyurethane system house in the Middle East producing raw material, for rigid, flexible, elastomeric and integral skin polyurethane foam. In 2006, Baalbaki started in-house production of Polyester Polyols which allowed for backward integration in its supply chain, giving Baalbaki a strong market position.

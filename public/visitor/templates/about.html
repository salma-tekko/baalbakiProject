Baalbaki at a glance

Baalbaki, corporation that occupies a prominent place in the economic history of Syria. It carried on a profitable Trade during the 20th century. In 1957, Omar Baalbaki and his son Ihsan founded Barada Metallic Industries. Clearly, the Baalbaki holds a diversified business portfolio and may pride itself of its past and present achievements. For Baalbaki, the principle of continuity has been invariably kinship which is critical to the success of the company.

What we do

Baalbaki engages in construction, Polyurethanes chemical industry, trade, real estate investments and agriculture.

Baalbaki serves the construction industry by providing products with a focus on insulation. This includes PU insulated sandwich panels and prefabricated houses, insulated building blocks and decorative plaster, in addition to EPS hourdy blocks for ceilings.

Baalbaki mattress industry offers high quality PU mattresses adapted to the requirements of the International humanitarian organizations along with a full range of comfort mattresses for local and export market.

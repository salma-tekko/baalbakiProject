CONSTRUCTION

Baalbaki’s activities in the construction industry are focused on prefabricated houses for the local market as well as humanitarian organizations. Baalbaki prefabricated houses include offices, workers housing, public and private showers and toilets, kiosks, and others.

In addition, Baalbaki offers PU insulated sandwich panels, decorative insulated plaster, water repellents, ceramic and tile adhesive, interlock and sidewalks to the building sector.

Prefabricated Houses

Prefabrication is not new. However, Baalbaki prefabricated houses add a completely new concept as we combine old-style modular concepts with a customized approach, allowing our customers to choose a basic design and adjust it according to their tastes and needs.

Our prefabricated houses are built on a lightweight steel skeleton surrounded and covered by pre-painted galvanized steel PU insulated sandwich panels; they can be easily assembled and dismantled and are suitable for transportation.

Light blocks, Interlocks, Sidewalks

In 2001, Baalbaki started a thorough research and development to innovate insulated hollow blocks used in the nonbearing walls in buildings. In 2007, we introduced Baalbaki EPS based Light Weight Insulated Cement Block and acquired a patent under the number 5619 dated 2007/6/18. In 2010, Baalbaki started producing the Sandwich Light Weight Blocks that are considered a perfect solution for thermal insulation, in addition to their high compressive strength.
